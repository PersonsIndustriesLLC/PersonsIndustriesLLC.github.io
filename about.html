Who We Are

Persons Industries LLC is a specialized engineering firm dedicated to delivering innovative solutions in autonomous systems, full‑stack software development, and mission‑critical applications.

We design, program, model, and simulate advanced autonomous systems and integrate AI with precision to solve complex engineering challenges. We also provide comprehensive services in electrical circuit design and refurbishment, UX‑centric website design, and detailed computer‑aided design (CAD) to support both product and software lifecycles.

We partner with defense contractors, technology innovators, and industrial organizations to design and implement systems that meet demanding performance and safety requirements. From AI‑enabled autonomous platforms and high‑fidelity software solutions to robust electrical and CAD engineering services, our team emphasizes technical precision, collaboration, and a mission‑first philosophy that ensures operational success.
